Design trends in men's sweatshirt

Zip-up hoodie design trends

Men's zip-up hoodies have become trendy for layering, streetwear, and smart-casual occasions.
Layering combines a zip-up hoodie with other clothes for style, comfort or warmth.
That's why men sometimes wear zip-up hoodies over a shirt while at work.
Sometimes they're worn over an untucked shirt on the streets.

Advantages and disadvantages of zip-up hoodies

Zip-up hoodies have two advantages:
they're suitable for work or a casual look and they provide a relaxed fit for added comfort.
Zip-up hoodies for men also have 2 drawbacks worth mentioning. Their zips can make layering more difficult. Plus,
they're less warm as the fit is less snug.

*Pull-over hoodie design trends
Men's pull-over hoodies feature in both the streetwear and athleisure trends.
In streetwear, a pull-over hoodie combines with clothes made from leather and denim.
In athleisure, a pull-over hoodie unites style with comfort for a functional look.
Pull-over hoodies for men are typically plain when worn for athleisure.
Other trends come into play for streetwear. Streetwear pull-over hoodies often come in colors that stand out.
Color gradients are also available. Many pull-over hoodies feature slogans, brand logos or intriguing patterns.
These provide more options for buyers.

Advantages and disadvantages of pull-over hoodies

Pull-over hoodies for men have 2 key advantages:
they feature drawstring hoods that keep out the rain and they provide a minimalist look.
There are also 2 specific disadvantages with men's pull-over hoodies.
They're not suitable for smart-casual or formal environments and they take extra time to put on and take off.

Target buyers for these design trends
Buyers of zip-up hoodies fit into different categories based on specific trends. They are normally young,
cultured and urban. Those layering zip-up hoodies for smart-casual purposes will focus on professional activities.
These buyers like using zip-up hoodies to look cool in public.
Pull-over hoodie buyers also differ based on the trends they follow. Buyers focused on style are young men,
like university students. Those choosing athleisure outfits are gym members and athletes who like to exercise in hoodies.
Crewneck sweatshirt buyers are like hoodie buyers, but with some key differences.
They'll be aiming at more casual outfits. Sometimes, they'll be fashion-conscious individuals who want to remain stylish.
They prefer crewneck sweatshirts because they make it easy to appear fashionable.

Next steps

The design trends of men's hoodies and sweatshirts are many and varied. Zip-up hoodies are appropriate for layering,
workwear or smart-casual activities. Pull-over hoodies get chosen for streetwear and casual occasions.
Crewneck sweatshirts get picked for retro themes, bright colors or use as sportswear.
These trends show the market potential for men's hoodies and sweatshirts. Picking a suitable item from the subcategories
above is a sensible business decision. Following these trends and understanding your customers' desires
will translate into good profits for your business.
